Injera little detail about Injera
Injera is made with teff, a tiny,
round grain that flourishes in the highlands of Ethiopia.
While teff is very nutritious, it contains practically no gluten.
This makes teff ill-suited for making raised bread,
however injera still takes advantage of the special properties of yeast.
